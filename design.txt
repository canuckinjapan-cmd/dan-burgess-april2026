# Design System: Dan Burgess Portfolio

## 1. Design Vision & Mood
"Bridging Western minimalism and Japanese trust-based design."
The aesthetic is clean, professional, and cross-border. It balances the raw, bold typography of Western modernism with the textural sensitivity and meticulous alignment of Japanese design.

## 2. Color Palette
- **Primary Background**: `#eff0f2` (Light grey base).
- **Foreground Text**: `#111111` (Deep charcoal/black).
- **Primary Accent**: `#f27d26` (Bright Orange - used for links, bullet points, and the custom cursor).
- **Secondary Surface**: `#ffffff` (Pure white for cards and high-contrast sections).
- **Texture**: Rice paper overlay image (`rice-paper-2.png`) is applied globally to add organic depth.

## 3. Typography
- **Display Header**: `Archivo`, sans-serif. Used for large section titles and high-impact headings.
- **Body Text**: `Source Sans 3`, sans-serif. Used for paragraphs and general UI text.
- **Serif Accent**: `Playfair Display`, serif. Used for artistic subtitles and process descriptions.
- **Interactive/UI**: `Zen Maru Gothic`. A rounded Japanese font used for the trailing hero cursor and the site favicon to convey friendliness and precision.
- **Japanese Support**: `Noto Sans JP`.

## 4. Layout & Spacing
- **Grid Strategy**: Fluid layout with responsive grid containers.
- **Section Padding**: Standard vertical padding of `100px` for desktop sections.
- **Card Corners**: Rounded corners with `20px` radius for service and portfolio items.
- **Header**: Fixed `60px` height with a glassmorphism (10px blur) background.

## 5. UI Components & Interactions
- **Hero Cursor**: A floating horizontal text trail (Zen Maru Gothic, 46px) that follows the user's mouse movement. Disabled on touch devices.
- **Stacking Cards**: The "Design Services" section uses a scroll-linked sticky stacking effect where cards slide from the left and overlap.
- **Ripple CTA**: The word "Click" in the portfolio subtitle has a staggered wave/ripple animation to draw attention.
- **Marquee Footer**: A horizontal scrolling text track using `Archivo` font at large scales (36px to 70px).
- **Neon Buttons**: Interaction-heavy buttons that feature a vertical sliding text transition on hover.

## 6. Icons
- **Icon Set**: Google Material Symbols (Outlined).
- **Primary Icon**: `south_east` (arrow) used for external link indicators.

## 7. Assets & Textures
- **Rice Paper**: `https://danburgess.com/2026-assets/rice-paper-2.png`
- **Favicon**: Orange character "団" (Zen Maru Gothic) on a black square with rounded visual weight.
