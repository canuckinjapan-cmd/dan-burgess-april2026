ABOUT ME

🍁 Canada Roots

My journey began in Canada with magazine layouts and sign design, blending traditional aesthetics with technical foundations. This early exposure to visual communication set the stage for a career dedicated to clarity and impact.

🇯🇵  Japan Precision

Based in Japan since 1992, I’ve localized Windows games in Nagano and coordinated UI production for global tech giants in Tokyo. This experience instilled a deep respect for technical precision and cross-cultural storytelling.

📱 Digital-first Design

Today, I specialize in No-Code development to bridge the gap between vision and launch. By removing technical friction, I help clients focus on user value and polished digital experiences that scale.

THE WORK
PROCESS

How I bridge the gap from vision to launch.

Discovery & Strategy

I begin by defining your goals and audience. I draw on my decades of cross-cultural experience to ensure your message resonates—whether your market is in Tokyo, Toronto, or beyond.

Visual Architecture

I translate your vision into a cohesive design system. From UI/UX flows to 3D visualization, I build a blueprint that prioritizes both aesthetic polish and functional clarity.

No-Code Development

Using industry-leading No-Code tools, I build your site in real-time. This eliminates the "black box" of traditional coding, allowing for faster iterations, lower overhead, and a highly flexible final product.

Refinement & Launch

I polish every micro-interaction and optimize for performance across all devices. The result is a high-end digital experience that you can easily manage and scale long after launch.
